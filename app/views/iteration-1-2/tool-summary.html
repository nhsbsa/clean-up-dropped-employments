What the tool cannot do yet

These are issues the tool cannot detect. We are working on fixes for them in future releases.

Null/blank field handling 1.2

Records with blank or null employment IDs or dates can be inaccurate. For example, it can produce misleading results or miss some record issues. This is most common with 'edge cases'.

False positive detection 1.2

Some records that were not updated may be incorrectly flagged as overlapping. These records are correct but appear wrong because of missed updates. The tool will soon distinguish between corrupted records and those that are correct but were not updated.

New corruption logic 1.2

There are emerging patterns the tool cannot detect yet. These include when statuses in tables do not match. Further corruption detection logic is needed for these newly identified failure modes.

Alignment and label consistency 1.2

The tool retrieves information. But the tables which organise this information have some inconsistent titles and labels. For example, look at the 'service history' and 'service group' tables in the results view. You will see the column headings are different in places.
